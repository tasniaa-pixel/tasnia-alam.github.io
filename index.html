About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the School of Communication at the University of Miami. My research examines public relations, political communication, social movements, crisis communication, and digital media, with a particular focus on the Global South. My current work explores diasporic strategic communication and public engagement during periods of political unrest and media restriction.
